Disadvantages

    A diet high in unhealthy foods can lead to obesity.
    A diet high in saturated fat and low in fiber can increase the risk of heart disease, stroke, and other heart-related problems.
    A poor diet can lead to high blood pressure, which can have serious consequences if left untreated.
    A poor intake of calcium, protein, and vitamin D can increase the risk of osteoporosis.
    A poor diet can also increase the risk of certain cancers, depression, and eating disorders
